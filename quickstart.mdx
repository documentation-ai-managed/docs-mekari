## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- A valid email address for account creation
- Team member emails ready for invitations
- Basic knowledge of Markdown for content creation

<Callout kind="info">

Mekari requires a modern browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari. Disable ad blockers to avoid interface issues.

</Callout>

## Create Your Mekari Account

Follow these steps to sign up and access your dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Navigate to `https://dashboard.example.com` and click **Sign Up**.

    Select your role: Admin or Member.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, full name, and company name.

    Verify your email via the confirmation link sent to your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Password" icon="lock">
    Create a strong password meeting these requirements:
    - At least 12 characters
    - Includes uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Invite Team Members

Collaborate by adding your team right away.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Team Settings" icon="users">
    From the dashboard, click **Team** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Invites" icon="mail">
    Enter email addresses and select roles (Admin, Editor, Viewer).

    Click **Send Invitations**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Permissions" icon="shield">
    Review pending invites and set workspace access levels.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">

Invites expire after 7 days. Resend if needed.

</Callout>

## Build Your First Documentation Space

Create and populate your initial doc space.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Space" icon="book-open">
    Click **New Space** and name it `Project Docs`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Pages" icon="plus">
    Create pages like `Introduction.mdx` and `API Reference.mdx`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Content" icon="edit-3">
    Use the editor to write in Markdown or MDX.

    Here's a basic example:

    <CodeGroup tabs="Markdown,MDX">
    ````markdown
# Welcome

This is your first doc page.
    ````

    ````mdx
## Overview

Welcome to your Mekari space.

<Callout kind="success">Content added successfully!</Callout>
    ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Integrate with API

Connect Mekari to your apps using the API.

<Request tabs="cURL,JavaScript" show-lines="true">
````curl
curl -X POST https://api.example.com/v1/spaces \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"name": "New Space", "description": "Automated space"}'
````

````javascript
const response = await fetch('https://api.example.com/v1/spaces', {
  method: 'POST',
  headers: {
    'Authorization': 'Bearer YOUR_API_KEY',
    'Content-Type': 'application/json',
  },
  body: JSON.stringify({
    name: 'New Space',
    description: 'Automated space'
  })
});
````
</Request>
