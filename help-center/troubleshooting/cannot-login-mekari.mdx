---
title: Cannot Log In to Mekari
description: Fix login issues quickly.
---

## Symptoms

- Unable to access account
- Error: "Invalid email or password"

## Solutions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    ````
    Use the "Forgot password?" link on the login page and follow the instructions.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify email">
    Check your inbox for the verification email and confirm your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Support">
    If issues persist, email support@mekari.com for assistance.
  </Step>
</Steps>
