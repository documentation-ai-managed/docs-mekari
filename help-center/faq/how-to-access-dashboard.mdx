## Overview

To start using Mekari, sign in to access your dashboard.

## Steps

1. Visit `https://dashboard.mekari.com`
2. Enter your registered email & password
3. Click "Login"
4. You are redirected to your dashboard

## Tips

- Use "Forgot password?" if you cannot log in
- Ensure your account is verified via email
