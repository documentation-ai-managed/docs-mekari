---
title: Inviting Team Members to Mekari
description: Quickly add new users to your project.
---

## Overview

Add team members so they can access your Mekari projects.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Team Settings">
    Navigate to the "Team" tab in your Mekari dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Invitation">
    ````
    Click "Invite Member", enter their email, and choose a role.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Team Member Accepts">
    Once the invite is received, your team member clicks the link to join your project.
  </Step>
</Steps>
